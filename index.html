Lot 01 · The Evening Sale

Untitled (Interior, Evening)

Marguerite Solano, b. 1978
MediumOil on linen
Executed2025
Dimensions116 × 89 cm (45⅝ × 35 in)
SignatureSigned and dated lower right
ConditionExcellent · report available on request
Estimate
$18,000 – 24,000
Buyer's premium and applicable taxes not included
Request a Condition Report Register to Bid

Description

A quiet, low-lit domestic interior rendered in Solano's characteristic close tonal range — umber, bone, and a single band of lamp-gold drawn across the far wall. The composition withholds more than it shows: a doorway left open onto darkness, a chair angled as though just vacated.

Painted during the artist's residency outside Lisbon, the work belongs to a small series exploring domestic space as a site of memory rather than habitation.

Provenance

  • The artist's studio, acquired directly by the present owner, 2025
  • Private collection, New York

Exhibited

  • Lisbon, Estúdio Aberto, "Rooms Without Names," 2025, no. 4

Specialist Note

This lot is offered directly from the artist's most recent series and has not previously appeared at auction. Solano's evening market has grown steadily since her 2023 institutional debut.

Description

Built up in successive translucent layers of wax-bound oil, the panel's surface reads almost as sediment — a threshold worn down rather than painted on. Ferrand works without preparatory drawing, allowing each pass of the scraper to determine the next.

Provenance

  • Galerie Non Standard, Brussels
  • Acquired by the present owner, 2024

Specialist Note

Part of a body of work first shown at the 2024 Brussels gallery weekend, where it drew early institutional interest.
